Work Experience

Modeling of heat extraction from depleted oil and gas wells

June'18-Present

I am working on numerical modeling of heat extraction from abandoned oil and gas wells for geothermal energy production. In the project, we have coupled thermal reservoir with bore well heat transfer and laminar flow model to perform numerical simulations using finite element method to assess the variation of production temperature with time and various operating parameters such as injection mass flow rate, reinjection temperature, well depth and geothermal gradient.

My research work has been accepted for presentation at the American Geophysical Union Fall Meeting 2018 and I have also been awarded the AGU student travel grant award.

The research work had also been accepted for presentation at McDonnell's 7th International Symposium at Tsinghua University, Beijing, China in October, 2018.

I hope to submit this work for a research journal publication by the end of Nov'18.
